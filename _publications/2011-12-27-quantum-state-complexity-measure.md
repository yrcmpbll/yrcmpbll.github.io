The paper proposes extending a classical complexity measure to quantum states in order to characterize quantum-state complexity beyond Kolmogorov-style algorithmic approaches. It applies the method to a mixed state constructed from a W-state with controlled white noise and reports convex behavior in the resulting quantum complexity measure.

Keywords: quantum state complexity, W-state, white noise, complexity quantification, quantum information

Recommended citation: Yuri Campbell and José Roberto Castilho Piqueira. (2011). &quot;Quantum State Complexity Measure.&quot; <i>arXiv preprint arXiv:1110.6128v1</i>.
