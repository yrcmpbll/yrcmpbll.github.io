The paper extends Shiner-Davison-Landsberg and López-Ruiz-Mancini-Calbet complexity measures to quantum information settings by using von Neumann entropy as a quantum disorder measure. It applies the resulting measures to simple qubit systems and Werner states, showing that the proposed framework reproduces classical-like behavior in basic cases and offers a way to discuss complexity in quantum states.

Keywords: quantum complexity, SDL complexity, LMC complexity, von Neumann entropy, Werner states
