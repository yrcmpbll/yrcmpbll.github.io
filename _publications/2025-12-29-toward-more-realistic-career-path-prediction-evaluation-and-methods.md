The paper evaluates career path prediction models under more realistic input conditions, including structured and free-text career histories. It compares linear projection, MLP, LSTM, and LLM-based approaches across public datasets, studies synthetic data and fine-tuning strategies, and provides qualitative analysis of prediction behavior. The work establishes new baselines and practical insights for deploying career path prediction systems.

Keywords: career path prediction, labor market NLP, synthetic data, LLM recommendation, model evaluation
