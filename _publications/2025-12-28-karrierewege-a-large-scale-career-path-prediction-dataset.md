The paper introduces KARRIEREWEGE, a public dataset of more than 500,000 career paths linked to the ESCO taxonomy. To address free-text resume inputs, it augments the dataset with synthetic job titles and descriptions, creating KARRIEREWEGE+. Benchmarks show that synthetic data improves performance and robustness for free-text career path prediction.

Keywords: career path prediction, large-scale dataset, ESCO taxonomy, synthetic resumes, HR recommender systems
