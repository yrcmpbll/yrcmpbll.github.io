The paper investigates whether company website texts can be used to identify technologically similar firms for R&amp;D partner discovery, especially for SMEs that may not be well represented in patents or publication databases. It applies NLP methods to website text and finds that BERT-based semantic representations outperform alternatives, reaching strong agreement with expert-validated similarity judgments.

Keywords: company similarity, website mining, BERT, R&amp;D partner selection, semantic similarity

Recommended citation: Jan-Peter Bergmann, Miriam Amin, Yuri Campbell, and Karl Trela. (2023). &quot;How to find similar companies using websites?.&quot; <i>World Patent Information 73, 102172</i>.
