The paper proposes a term-centric framework for inducing interpretable hierarchical taxonomies from heterogeneous corpora. Instead of relying on whole-document representations, it maps documents into a shared term-based representation space using automatic term extraction, supporting cross-source alignment and scalable hierarchy construction. Experiments on a large English/German benchmark and a German regional innovation case study show improvements in cross-source coherence and hierarchy quality over text- and summary-based baselines.

Keywords: taxonomy induction, term-centric representations, heterogeneous corpora, automatic term extraction, innovation mapping
