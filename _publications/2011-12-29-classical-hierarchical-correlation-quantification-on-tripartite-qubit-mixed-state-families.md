The paper studies how irreducibility and correlation structure can be quantified in tripartite qubit mixed states. It uses statistics from von Neumann measurements over mutually unbiased bases to estimate hierarchical information and tests the approach on families representing different tripartite entanglement structures. Results indicate sensitivity to different kinds of entanglement.

Keywords: hierarchical correlations, tripartite qubits, quantum entanglement, mutually unbiased bases, quantum information

Recommended citation: Yuri Campbell and José Roberto Castilho Piqueira. (2011). &quot;Classical Hierarchical Correlation Quantification on Tripartite Qubit Mixed State Families.&quot; <i>arXiv preprint arXiv:1110.6128</i>.
