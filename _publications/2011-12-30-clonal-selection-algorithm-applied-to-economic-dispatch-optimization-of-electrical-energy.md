The chapter applies the CLONALG clonal selection metaheuristic to the economic dispatch problem in power systems. It formulates the optimization using Karush-Kuhn-Tucker-based objective conditions and tests whether the method can find cost-optimal generation schedules while respecting generation constraints.

Keywords: economic dispatch, clonal selection algorithm, power systems optimization, metaheuristics, Karush-Kuhn-Tucker conditions
