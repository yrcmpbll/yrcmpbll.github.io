The paper extends a computational-mechanics-inspired complexity measure to quantum state complexity. It applies the method to Werner states and reports a transition between entangled and separable regimes, suggesting a relationship between complexity robustness and entanglement. Symbolic dynamics analysis is used to distinguish separable and entangled structural behavior.

Keywords: Werner state, quantum entanglement, phase transition, computational mechanics, symbolic dynamics

Recommended citation: Yuri Campbell and José Roberto Castilho Piqueira. (2011). &quot;Quantum Entanglement Phase Transition in Werner State.&quot; <i>arXiv preprint arXiv:1110.6129</i>.
