The paper evaluates a Takagi-Sugeno fuzzy inference system for calibrating digital electronic compasses. It treats compass calibration as a nonlinear modeling and error-reduction problem, filters measurement noise before applying the fuzzy system, and reports substantial reduction in total acquisition error in the tested setting.

Keywords: digital compass calibration, Takagi-Sugeno fuzzy system, fuzzy inference, sensor error reduction, nonlinear system modeling
